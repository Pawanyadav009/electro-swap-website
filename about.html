About US

ElectroSwap is a platform developed by Pawan from Dream Different Company, with a mission to make buying and selling electronics simple and secure. We believe that every gadget has a story, and we want to help give those stories a new life.
Our goal is to build a trusted community where people can easily sell their pre-owned gadgets and find quality electronics in good condition. We prioritize safety and transparency in every transaction, ensuring you can buy and sell with complete peace of mind.
We help you find a new home for your old gadgets and ensure that the cycle of technology continues. Welcome to ElectroSwap.
